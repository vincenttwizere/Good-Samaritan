OUR SERVICES

In Kigali, Rwanda, we address the dual challenge of food waste and scarcity. While restaurants discard surplus food,
many children's homes lack resources to meet dietary needs. Join us in minimizing waste and supporting those in need.

Food wastage and environmental impact
